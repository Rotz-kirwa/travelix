About Wildwave Safaris

Your premier safari and adventure travel partner, crafting extraordinary wildlife experiences and cultural adventures across the globe.

Our Safari Legacy

Founded with a passion for African wildlife and adventure, Wildwave Safaris has established itself as Kenya's leading safari and adventure travel company. We specialize in authentic wildlife experiences that connect travelers with nature's most spectacular displays, from the Great Migration to intimate gorilla encounters.

Our commitment to conservation and authentic experiences has earned us recognition from wildlife enthusiasts worldwide. We don't just organize safaris; we create transformative wildlife encounters that inspire conservation and forge deep connections with Africa's incredible ecosystems.

Our Mission

To create extraordinary safari and adventure experiences that connect travelers with Africa's incredible wildlife while promoting conservation, supporting local communities, and maintaining the highest standards of responsible tourism.

Our Vision

To be Africa's most trusted safari company, recognized for our authentic wildlife experiences, conservation leadership, and commitment to creating transformative adventures that inspire wildlife protection and cultural understanding.

Our Core Values
